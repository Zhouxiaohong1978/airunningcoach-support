  • Account Info: Email address provided at registration, used for login and account recovery.
  • Usage Data: Training plan generation counts and other usage statistics for free quota management.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide run tracking and training plan features
  • Generate personalized AI training advice
  • Manage subscription status and free usage quotas
  • Improve App features and user experience

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share data with third parties in the following cases:
